In this era of digital connectivity, a personal website serves as a contemporary business card, eliminating the environmental cost while providing enhanced reach, interactivity, and dynamism. With this notion in mind, I embarked on a journey to create my own corner of the web. The decision was not solely driven by the practicality of having an online presence but also fueled by the prospect of diving into the realm of web development – a domain I had yet to explore.

AAs someone with some background in programming, having delved into languages such as JavaScript, Visual Basic, MATLAB, and Java during my educational journey, the prospect of web development held an intriguing allure. Reflecting on my initial exposure to web technologies during my early teenage years, I recall grappling with HTML tables—an uninspiring introduction (In 2010, HTML schooling was in its early prototype phases) that failed to convey the creative possibilities that web development held.

An amazing 12 years — from the initial introduction of HTML in school curriculum to the contemporary era of AI-assisted rapid website creation.

Fast forward to the present, and my foray into website creation revealed itself as a captivating learning expedition. This article unfolds not just as a technical guide but as a narrative of exploration, documenting the unexpected twists and turns that the journey took. It's about the challenges and triumphs, the code and creativity, and the evolution of my perspective on web design.

The journey brought forth a newfound respect for web designers, and the nuanced relationship forged with AI tools like ChatGPT, and contemplation on the future landscape of personal websites and business cards in the era of advancing artificial intelligence. This isn't just a how-to; it's a narrative of my exploration, an ode to the evolving landscape of web development in the era of artificial intelligence.
